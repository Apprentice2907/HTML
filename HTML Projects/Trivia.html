Science Facts

  • Did you know? Water can boil and freeze at the same time! This is called the triple point.
  • There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all Earth's beaches.
  • Humans share about 60% of their DNA with bananas!

History Facts

  1. The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar on 27 August 1896. It lasted only 38 minutes.
  2. The Eiffel Tower can be 15 cm taller during the summer due to the expansion of metal in the heat.
  3. In ancient Egypt, servants were sometimes covered in honey to attract flies away from the pharaoh.
