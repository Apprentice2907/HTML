Featured Recipe: Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Recipe Information

Preparation Time:
Marination: 4-6 hours
Cooking Time:
45 minutes
Servings:
6 portions
Spice Level:
Medium Spicy

Ingredients

For Marination
For Gravy

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate chicken
    Chef's Tip

    For best results, marinate overnight in refrigerator

  2. Cook marinated chicken in tandoor or oven
  3. Prepare makhani gravy
  4. Combine chicken with gravy
  5. Finish with cream and butter
